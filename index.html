Internet Banking · BRI · 2024

QLola Internet Banking Bisnis

BRI’s business internet banking platform received feedback that users struggled during transfers — due to poor form design, lack of validation, and distracting UI elements. This case study outlines the problems, solutions, and impact of improving the transaction experience.

Problem
Saved Account Quick Selection
Users with many saved accounts had to scroll through a long list to find the right one.
Lack of Immediate Validation
Account details could not be verified early, increasing user anxiety.
Distracting Elements
Exchange rate cards, articles, and banners appeared during the transaction process.
No Immediate Receipt Access
Users were unsure the transaction succeeded because the receipt did not appear immediately.
Solved
Saved Account Pop-up
Show a saved accounts pop-up when entering BRI Transfer. Enable search by name or number. Tabs: Favorites, Recent, All.
Inline Account Validation
Tambah tombol "Cek Rekening" setelah input. Nama dan bank terverifikasi langsung muncul.
Limit Notification & Feature Guide
Show a card if the amount exceeds the limit. A guide on how to raise the limit is available.
Real-Time Receipt with Action Options
Show the receipt with options to Save, Share, and Download. Includes a success animation.

Mobile App · Spiritual · 2023

Gohaji Umroh

Gohaji Umroh is a Hajj & Umrah service provider focused on enhancing every pilgrim’s spiritual journey. With a strong commitment to quality and authenticity, every aspect of the service is designed to ensure a seamless, Sunnah-aligned experience.

Problem
No Official Website
There was no centralized service hub, making it difficult for prospective pilgrims to find accurate information.
Scattered Package Information
Umrah package information was unstructured and difficult for users to compare.
No Clear User Journey
There was no clear flow encouraging users to move toward a consultation.
Lack of Trust Elements
No testimonials, company profile, or social proof to build user trust.
High Friction
Users had to reach out manually for basic information — creating major friction before conversion.
Solved
Structured Page Hierarchy
Structured the website’s page hierarchy to improve information accessibility and reduce drop-off.
Landing Page with Strong Value Proposition
Designed the landing page with a clear value proposition and strategic CTA placement to increase consultation intent.
Standardized Package Format
Presented Umrah packages in a clear, standardized format to speed up user decision-making.
WhatsApp as Primary Channel
Directed users to WhatsApp as the primary consultation channel — fast, familiar, and frictionless.

Web Design · Career · 2022

IT's Buah Career Website

A career website makes it easy for applicants and companies to find each other — without limits of space or time. It also builds company branding and presents information easily for prospective applicants.

Research Planning
Objective
Memudahkan pelamar & perusahaan menemukan satu sama lain tanpa batasan ruang dan waktu.
Methodology
Qualitative in-depth interview & Usability Testing.
Respondents
Male/Female · Age 18–25 · Education SMA–S1 · Previously applied for jobs online.
Problem
No Job Listings
Before the redesign, the website listed no open positions for recruitment.
UI Didn’t Represent the Brand
The interface did not reflect the company’s identity or the team behind it.
Budaya Companies Tidak Terlihat
IT's Buah wanted to introduce company culture to applicants via Instagram content — but it wasn’t integrated.
Position Type Unclear
Open positions didn’t specify whether they were full-time, internship, or freelance.
Solved
Centralized Career Page
Created a centralized career page clearly listing all open positions, easy to discover.
Structured Job Categories
Organized listings by Full-time, Internship, and Freelance so applicants can easily understand the type of opportunity offered.
Complete Position Details
Each position includes job descriptions, responsibilities, and requirements — helping candidates evaluate fit before applying.
Culture & Instagram Section
Highlights company culture, team activities, and Instagram content to give candidates a sense of the workplace environment.

API Portal · Developer · 2023

BRIAPI Developer Portal

A BRI API solution enabling businesses and merchants to manage virtual accounts seamlessly — without complex IT infrastructure. This case study focuses on transforming the visual and developer experience from purely functional to aspirational.

Problem
Overly Technical Appearance
The previous interface felt overly technical and lacked visual appeal, failing to reflect the innovation and strength of the API product.
Weak Landing Experience
The initial experience wasn’t compelling enough to attract developers or potential business users to explore further — leaving a flat impression.
Unclear Value Proposition
BRIAPI’s key advantages — such as ease of integration and scalability — were not clearly communicated upfront.
Hero Section Lacked Impact
The hero section lacked sufficient visual and messaging strength to capture user attention within the first few seconds.
Solved
Bold & Modern Hero Visual
Redesigned the hero section with bold, high-contrast visuals and dynamic energy for a strong, modern first impression.
Cinematic Approach
Introduced a more cinematic visual approach to better represent BRIAPI’s speed, strength, and scalability.
Strong Visual Hierarchy
Restructured headlines and visual hierarchy so the core value proposition is instantly understood at a glance.
Aspirational Experience
Shifted the experience from purely functional to emotional and aspirational — encouraging developers to explore documentation and start integrating.
